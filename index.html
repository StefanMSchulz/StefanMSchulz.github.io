Deep Learning for Geoscience Data

During my work as a student research assistant under PD Dr. Steinhage and Florian Huber I worked on partial convolutions applied to satellite data to infer missing temperature data. Our approach proved to outperform the current state-of-the-art methods by producing more accurate and complete temperature maps. You can check the publication at MDPI.
